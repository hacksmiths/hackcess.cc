Improving lives with Hacking

Accessibility Hackathon

21-22 November, 2015

Goldsmiths College, New Cross, London

About

The Event

This hackathon is focused on improving the life of people with disabilitys, improving accessability by using cutting-edge technology. We'll provide you with food, drink, electricity, WiFi, 3D printers and tech so that you can get on with the real work - changing lives!

The Venue

Hackcess will take place in Goldsmiths University's St James Hatcham Church, a stunning refurbished church.
